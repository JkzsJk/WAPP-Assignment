Ducky Shine DKSH1408SD (Product Code:A004)

Introducing Ducky's newest mechanical keyboard for 2014. This is an update to the venerable Shine series with a modern look. With the introduction of dual color LEDs, our backlighting effects are even more colorful than before. Ducky Shine 4 continue to use the familiar Cherry MX mechanical key switches for unrivaled reliability and consistency between key presses.
